Welcome one and all to my personal website - MJHd.info... I go by the name MJHd or MJHdesigner if you prefer, but you can just call me Matt if you like. I have been a graphic designer since my junior year of highschool, and have spent the past several years mastering frontend design and dynamic web development.

Since learning HTML at Palomar College in my early design days, I always had a strong affinity for the challenge of web design. Like print design there is a blank canvas element that is intoxicating but also a technical aspect that is always pushing into progression. Since the early days my focus has been largly on new technology, web standards, and mastering the more complex aspects of web development.

I now split my time, freelancing between graphic design and web development. Which is just perfect for me because I'm not a big fan of compromise when it comes to what I want to accomplish. Now a days it's perfectly normal for me to spend half a day in illustrator, and the other half on notepad++ debugging PHP scripts in XAMPP...

So for some info about me personally, I live in San Diego, CA. When I'm not working or stomping out open challenges on Codecademy, you can find me at Grandview or OB pier surfing or skating the new park in Encinitas. I have soon to be 7 year old son named Brad boy who is arguably the raddest kid in the world, and a cat affectionately named OC (Outside Cat).

So that's enough about me - please take a look around my site, make yourself at home, and if you're feeling it shoot me a line and tell me a bit about you!

Thanks for visiting :D -MJHd
