Battle Royale is a playful comparison staged as a "battle" between various web technologies organized into two groups: Camp HTML5 and The jQuery Alliance. This was my final projects from SDCE's Web Specialist program... It's always been one of my favorite projects because of its unique slider in slider layout and the use of so many different visual styles in one project. That and it was fun as hell to make...
